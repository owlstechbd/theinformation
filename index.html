Why your company should migrate to Tableau Cloud

Eliminate the costs and time spent managing servers. With Tableau Cloud, your team can focus on insights, not infrastructure.

Effortlessly adapt to your organization’s needs. Tableau Cloud offers built-in scalability, robust security, and governance to support any workload

Stay ahead of the curve by aligning with a cloud-first strategy. Tableau Cloud empowers your organization to embrace future-ready data practices.

About the product

CloudSure

The Information Lab created CloudSure, a complete pipeline that allows you to move your content from tableau server to tableau cloud in which you will be driven and supported not only in the migration phase but on different ways as:

  • The engagement of your IT team
  • Understanding of feasibility
  • Helping you in the resolution of the errors during the migration
  • Proposing you a training
  • Supporting you with a Level2 support on HyperCare

A PRO is that with Project Mimic and TIL you can migrate from tableau server to tableau cloud in weeks instead of months and at the same time you can spend time upskilling your team not to manage TS and his infrastructure but on other activities
